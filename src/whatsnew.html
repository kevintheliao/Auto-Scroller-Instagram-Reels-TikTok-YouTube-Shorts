Auto Scroller v2.1.3

Now on TikTok & YouTube Shorts

Auto Scroller is no longer just for Instagram Reels. Same hands-free scrolling, two new platforms.

NEW

TikTok support

When a video ends, the next one scrolls into view automatically — no more looping. F likes, C opens comments, M mutes.

NEW

YouTube Shorts support

Shorts advance to the next video automatically when they finish. Press E to like without reaching for the mouse.

Keyboard shortcuts

Instagram & TikTok

  • Toggle Auto ScrollR
  • LikeF
  • Open CommentsC
  • Mute/UnmuteM

YouTube Shorts

  • Toggle Auto ScrollR
  • LikeE
  • Mute/UnmuteM
  • CaptionsC
  • FullscreenF

On YouTube, M, C and F are YouTube's own built-in shortcuts.
